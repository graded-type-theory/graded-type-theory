  completeness‚áâ (snd·µ¢ t) ‚ä¢t =

  completeness‚áâ (prodrec·µ¢ C t u) ‚ä¢t =
    let F , G , q , ‚ä¢F , ‚ä¢G , ‚ä¢C , ‚ä¢t , ‚ä¢u , A‚â°Ct = inversion-prodrec ‚ä¢t

        B , t‚áâB , Œ£FG‚â°B = completeness‚áâ t ‚ä¢t
        F‚Ä≤ , G‚Ä≤ , B‚áíŒ£‚Ä≤ , F‚â°F‚Ä≤ , G‚â°G‚Ä≤ = Œ£Norm (proj‚ÇÅ (soundness‚áâ (wfTerm ‚ä¢t) t‚áâB)) (sym Œ£FG‚â°B)
        u‚ááC‚Çä = completeness‚áá u (stabilityTerm ((reflConEq (wf ‚ä¢F)) ‚àô F‚â°F‚Ä≤ ‚àô G‚â°G‚Ä≤) ‚ä¢u)

                 stability

  completeness‚áâ ‚Ñï·µ¢ ‚ä¢t = _ , ‚Ñï·µ¢ , inversion-‚Ñï ‚ä¢t
